A Library typically refers to a place or a space, housing a large collection of books, study material or information. On Sunbird, library is one of the key modules where all content created or uploaded.



Prerequisite

Step Screen
  1. Login with your registered credential
  2. Click on the Library tab

Search

Step Screen
  1. Click the Dropdown
  2. Select from the following lists:
    • All: To search for all content available in the portal
    • Courses: To search for all the courses available in the portal
    • Library: To search for content available in the library
    • Organizations: To search for organizations uploaded in the portal
    • Users: To search for users uploaded in the portal
  3. Alternatively, you can type and search for the content in the library
    • Enter the name or keyword of the content in the Search Bar
    • Click on the search icon or press Enter

Advance Filter

Step Screen
  1. Click on the Filter icon
  2. You can filter the content by selecting from the respective dropdowns:
    • Curriculum
    • Content Types
    • Medium
    • Subjects
    • Concepts
  3. Alternatively, you can type and search for the content in the library
    • Click Apply to apply the filters
    • Click Reset to reset the selected filters

Sort content

Step Screen
  1. Click on the Dropdown option on the right
  2. Sort content based on latest Modified or Created

View Popular Stories

Popular stories are listed here. The different story types displayed are:

  • YouTube videos
  • Audio Clips
  • Video Clips
  • Assessments
  • Tutorials
  • Presentations
  • Worksheets
Step Screen
  1. Click on any of the story to view
  2. Click right or left Scroll bar to scroll

Consuming a Story

Step Screen
  • Click on the story you wish to consume. The story is displayed on the inbuilt content player. Description and content information of the story are provided below the player

Sharing Stroies

Step Screen
  1. Click on the Share icon to share the story
  1. A screen with the link to the story is displayed
  2. Click COPY LINK to copy the link
  3. Click Close icon to go back to the content player

Flagging Stories

Step Screen
  1. Click Flag icon to flag the displayed content
  1. Select a reason to describe why the content should be reviewed again
  2. Add additional comments, if any
  3. Click Submit to submit the reasons for flagging the content
  4. Click Close icon to exit the page

Taking Notes

Step Screen
  1. Click TAKE NOTE icon or link to take notes while viewing the story
  1. Enter Title
  2. Enter Description
  3. You can format the description entered with the various formatting options available
  4. Click Clear to clear the note
  5. Click Save to save the note
  6. Click Close icon to exit the page
